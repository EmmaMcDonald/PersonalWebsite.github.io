Lazy in Space

Bee and PuppyCat: Lazy In Space is an upcoming Web series coming in 2022 and is a sequel to Bee and PuppyCat. It is also created by Natasha Allegri. While originally planned to release in 2019, the series was delayed until 2022, with only the first episode premiering at Ottawa Animation Festival in 2019. In October of 2020 it was officially revealed to be coming to Netflix sometime in 2022
